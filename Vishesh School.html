Mission & Vision

VISHESH HAS SETUP WITH AIM OF PROVIDING AN EARLY INTERVENTION AND CARE PROGRAM FOR SPECIAL NEEDS CHILDREN WITH DISABILITIES AND TO THOSE NEEDING EDUCATIONAL, SOCIAL AND BEHAVIORAL COUNSELING.

What our Happy Parents Say

Ved being the most hyper kid in the entire family, where everyone gave up on Ved’s behavior issues would ever be able to improve… but since the time Ved has been going to Anoora, we have seen a remarkable change in his behavior and for that matter not just behavior but academics wise as well he has improved a lot. We are very much thankful to the Directors for their excellent customized curriculum and the teachers for implementing it on Ved and making him a different person all together.

Jigneh Gajjar
